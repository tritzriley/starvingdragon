What is Chili Crisp?

The Condiment That Changes Everything

Chili crisp is a Chinese condiment born in Sichuan province — an oil drizzled hot over dry chilis, aromatics, and spices that deliver a flavor unlike anything else: savory, spicy, crunchy, and deeply addictive.

🌶️

The Heat

Not just hot — layered. A slow-building, complex heat that lingers pleasantly rather than overwhelms. Our Sichuan chilies are toasted dry, then hit with hot oil to bloom their full depth — not just fire.

✨

The Tingle

That signature Sichuan "má" sensation — a gentle numbing tingle from Sichuan peppercorns — opens your palate and amplifies every other flavor on your tongue.

🥄

The Crunch

Crispy bits suspended in oil — toasted sesame, dried chili flakes, and spices — give every spoonful a satisfying texture that hot sauce simply can't match.

Hot Oil Drizzle Method

100% dry spices hit with hot oil at the perfect moment — no wet pastes, no shortcuts. The heat blooms every spice instantly for maximum flavor.

Sichuan Peppercorn

Facing Heaven Chilies

Toasted Sesame

Pairing Ideas

The beauty of chili crisp is its total shamelessness. It belongs on almost everything.

🥚

Eggs, Any Style

Soft-scrambled, fried, poached — chili crisp is the upgrade every egg has been waiting for.

🍜

Noodles & Ramen

Stir into instant ramen or hand-pulled noodles for a restaurant-level upgrade in 30 seconds.

🍕

Pizza

Drizzle over hot pizza instead of red pepper flakes. You will never go back. Ever.

🥗

Salads & Bowls

Use as a dressing base. Nutty, spicy, complex — makes grain bowls actually exciting.

🥟

Dumplings

The classic Sichuan pairing. Spoon over boiled wontons with a splash of black vinegar.

🍗

Grilled Meats

Brush on chicken, pork, or beef. Instant Sichuan street-food energy, zero effort.

🧀

Cheese Board

Drizzle over a cheese board. The heat cuts through richness in a way that feels like magic.

🫘

Beans & Tofu

Silken tofu + chili crisp + soy sauce = dinner. Transform bland proteins instantly.
